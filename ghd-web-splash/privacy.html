Privacy Policy

At GDriver, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

GDriver does not collect personal information unless explicitly shared by you. The app may store ride metrics, usage data, and trip statistics on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any data collected is used solely to enhance your experience with the app. Your data is not shared with third parties without your consent.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously. All data is securely stored on your device, and we employ encryption to protect sensitive information.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at support@gdriver.app.
